Creating Refresh Date Table 14



1. Transform Data: On the Power BI Desktop ribbon, click ‘Transform Data’.
2. Create New Source: Click on the ‘New Source’ button, select ‘Blank Query’ from the list, and click on ‘Connect’.
3. Name the Query: Under properties, type ‘Refresh Date’ in the Name box.
4. Enter DAX Formula: On the DAX formula bar, type =DateTime.LocalNow().
5. Convert to Table: Click on the ‘Convert to Table’ button.
6. Rename Column: After the refresh table appears, right-click on the column to rename it. Choose ‘Rename’ from the selection.
7. Save Changes: Click on the ‘Close & Apply’ button and save your changes.
