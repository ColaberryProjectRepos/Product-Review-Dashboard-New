Dataset Link: Dataset Link:  https://data.world/datafiniti/amazon-and-best-buy-electronics/workspace/file?filename=DatafinitiElectronicsProductData.csv


  1. Save Your Dataset: Ensure that your dataset is saved on your device.
  2. Open Power BI Desktop: Access the Power BI Desktop application.
  3. Navigate to Data: Click on the ‘Get Data’ ribbon.
  4. Select Text/CSV: Choose the ‘Text/CSV’ option, then select your data.
  5. Choose ‘DatafinitiElectronicsProductData’: Navigate to and select ‘DatafinitiElectronicsProductData.csv’, then click ‘Open’.
  6. Preview and Load Data: Select the table to display a data preview, then click ‘Load’.
